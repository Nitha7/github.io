About Me

Hello! I’m Nitha Cherian, a passionate front-end developer from Kochi, Kothamangalam. I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Applications (BCA) from Depaul College, Angamaly, and a Master’s degree in Computer Applications (MCA) from St. Teresa's College, Ernakulam. My journey in the world of technology didn’t stop there. I further honed my skills by completing a comprehensive Bootcamp course program at Aitrich Technologies, Thrissur.

Equipped with a strong foundation in web development, my skill set includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, TypeScript, and Angular. I’m dedicated to creating responsive, user-friendly, and visually appealing web experiences. I enjoy every part of the creative process, from planning and designing to implementation and optimization.

I’m excited about the opportunity to continue learning, growing, and contributing to the ever-evolving field of web development. Let’s connect and build something amazing together!
